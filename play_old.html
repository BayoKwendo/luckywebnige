    HOW TO PLAY LUCKYBOX

     

      LUCKY BOX

    LuckyBox is a Nigerian lottery game where you stand a chance to win cash prizes when you play. You have six boxes to chose from, three of the six have a cash value attached and three do not. Pick any box from 1-6 to win instantly by staking a minimum of Ksh 20. You do not have to wait for a draw! The power of winning is in your hands 24 hours a day, any hour, any minute you can be a winner. You can now play LuckyBox via SMS platform or on the Website.

     

    On the main page of our website,scroll down.Input your mobile number and upon a successful submission, you will recieve a notification message with simple instructions on how to play.

     

    To register, SMS PLAY to the short code 29303. You will receive a confirmation message acknowledging your successful registration.

     

    To Deposit to your LuckyBox Account go to Mpesa> Lipa na Mpesa> PayBill> Enter Business Number> 290813> Account Name > LUCKY

     

    Send Box Number to 29303. Example: 4 to 29303

     

    To Withdraw from your LuckyBox Account send W#amount to 29303. Example W#50 to 29303

     

    To check the Balance in your LuckyBox Account send BAL to 29303
